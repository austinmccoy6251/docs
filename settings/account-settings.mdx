# Account Settings

Manage your personal account settings and preferences in the Artemis Search platform.

## Accessing Account Settings

1. Log in to your Artemis Search account.
2. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner.
3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu.

## Available Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Profile Information">
    Update your basic profile information:
    - Name
    - Email address
    - Profile picture

    <Note>
      Changing your email address will require re-verification.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password">
    Change your account password:
    1. Enter your current password
    2. Enter your new password
    3. Confirm your new password

    <Tip>
      Use a strong, unique password to secure your account.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notification Preferences">
    Customize how and when you receive notifications:
    - Email notifications
    - In-app notifications
    - Project updates
    - Billing alerts
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Linked Accounts">
    Manage third-party accounts linked to your Artemis Search account:
    - Google
    - GitHub
    - Slack

    <Info>
      Linking accounts can enable additional features and integrations.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Delete Account

If you wish to delete your account, please follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Deletion">
    Scroll to the bottom of the Account Settings page and click on "Delete Account".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Read through the implications of account deletion and confirm your decision.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Feedback">
    Optionally, provide feedback on why you're deleting your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final Confirmation">
    Enter your password to confirm the account deletion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Account deletion is irreversible. All your data and projects will be permanently removed.
</Warning>
